About Us

Sweet and Steamy, Unique Taste House is a leading food manufacturing company based in the heart of Abuja. We specialize in producing authentic Nigerian food products that capture the rich, diverse flavors of our heritage. Our state-of-the-art facility in Lugbe combines traditional cooking methods with modern food safety standards, ensuring every product meets the highest quality benchmarks.

Our commitment goes beyond manufacturing – we're dedicated to preserving Nigerian culinary traditions while making them accessible to homes and businesses across the country. Every recipe is carefully crafted, every ingredient meticulously sourced, and every batch produced with pride.

At Sweet and Steamy, we understand that great food brings people together. That's why we put our heart into every product, ensuring that each taste tells a story of quality, authenticity, and the unique flavors that make Nigerian cuisine truly special.

Our Products & Services

Premium Food Manufacturing

We produce a wide range of authentic Nigerian food products including spice blends, seasoning mixes, ready-to-cook sauces, and traditional soup bases. Each product is manufactured to exacting standards in our certified facility.

Custom Recipe Development

Working with restaurants, hotels, and food businesses, we develop custom food products tailored to your specific requirements. From concept to production, we bring your culinary vision to life.

Bulk Supply & Distribution

We supply food products in bulk to retailers, wholesalers, and institutional buyers across Abuja and beyond. Our efficient distribution network ensures timely delivery and product freshness.

Quality Assurance & Food Safety

Every product undergoes rigorous quality control testing. We maintain NAFDAC compliance and implement international food safety standards to guarantee the safety and quality of our products.

Private Label Manufacturing

Launch your own food brand with our private label services. We handle formulation, production, and packaging while you focus on building your brand identity in the Nigerian market.
